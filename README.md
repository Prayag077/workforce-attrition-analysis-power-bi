## Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition to identify key factors contributing to workforce turnover. The dashboard provides actionable insights to support data-driven HR and workforce planning decisions.

## Objectives
- Measure overall attrition rate  
- Identify high-attrition departments and job roles  
- Analyze attrition trends by age, income, and experience  
- Support effective employee retention strategies  

## Dataset
The dataset includes employee-level information such as:
- Demographics (Age, Gender, Marital Status)  
- Job details (Department, Job Role, Job Level)  
- Compensation (Monthly Income)  
- Experience metrics  
- Attrition status (Yes / No)  

## Key KPIs
- Total Employees  
- Attrition Count  
- Attrition Rate (%)  
- Average Age  
- Average Monthly Income  
- Average Years at Company

## Key Insights
- Higher attrition observed among early-career employees  
- Lower income levels show increased attrition  
- Specific departments and roles experience higher turnover  

## Outcome
The analysis helps HR teams identify attrition risks and design targeted strategies to improve employee retention.
